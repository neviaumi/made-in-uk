New look, same recipes! We’re currently making some small changes to our packaging labels and have used this opportunity to give our packaging design a swanky new look too.

The fifth cat nap of the day can wait. This delicious dry cat food recipe is made with 65% freshly prepared chicken and 5% chicken liver. Oh, and there’s no meat meal or rendered meat in there, our recipes are just made with freshly prepared proper meat and offal. Along with natural ingredients and added vitamins and minerals. AKA, the good stuff. Which means your feline friend can enjoy tasty grub that’ll help nourish them inside and out. Sign us up.

Since cats love to graze, we recommended either leaving it out for them to snack on throughout the day or feeding alongside our wet food for those fussier felines (we see you).

  • Made with proper meat and offal
  • Made with natural ingredients
  • Complete and balanced
  • For adult cats from 6 months+
  • Feed in line with our feeding guidelines
