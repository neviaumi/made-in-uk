    Product description

    Balanced dry food for cats, with 100% natural ingredients and no grains or gluten, with chicken and salmon as the key ingredients, rich in protein and a source of taurine and omega-3 fatty acids.

    Encore Cat Chicken with Salmon is a delicious dry food for cats, made with 100% natural ingredients. The main ingredients are chicken and salmon, which form over 80% of the recipe and provide essential protein. This kibble contains no grains, gluten or other allergies, making it an ideal choice as a delectable hypoallergenic option for more sensitive cats. This food is easy to digest and contains potato as a digestible source of carbohydrate and fibre.
    This Encore Cat Chicken with Salmon provides omega-3 fatty acids from salmon oil, which helps to keep skin and coat healthy as well as supporting healthy brain function. It provides your cat with essential vitamins, minerals and taurine, a vital amino acid for supporting heart and vision. Encore Cat Chicken with Salmon also contains yucca schidigera to help reduce unpleasant stool odour.

    Encore Cat Chicken with Salmon at a glance:
    • Delicious dry food for cats
    • Protein-rich: with a high content of chicken and salmon
    • Hypoallergenic: free from allergens such as grains and gluten
    • Natural ingredients: free from artificial flavour enhancers and preservatives
    • Contains omega-3 fatty acids: to support skin and coat, as well as maintain healthy brain function
    • With brown algae: natural source of minerals
    • Source of taurine: essential amino acid for healthy heart function and strong vision
    • Contains yucca schidigera: can help to reduce unpleasant stool odour
    • Balanced recipe: suitable as a complete food for daily feeding
