This is a nutritionally complete pet food for adult cats 6months plus. It has everything your cat needs and is full of natural ingredients with added vitamins and minerals to help support their health. Serve with a bowl of fresh water nearby. Your cat may eat more or less depending on age, temperament and activity. Don't be fooled if your cat doesn't finish the dish. Cats regulate their own appetite, therefore it is usual for them to come back and graze.

At Lily's Kitchen they believe that the majority of a cat's diet should be made up of wet food as it more closely replicates a cat's natural diet. They therefore recommend you feed their delicious dry food as a companion to their wet food for added crunch and variety.
